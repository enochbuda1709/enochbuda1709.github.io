Naming creation.

There are countless brands and brands in consumer markets, with a wide variety of names, word combinations and acronyms involved. Therefore, it seems to brand managers that in order to stand out in the market from competitors, it is necessary to come up with something incredible and fantastic. In fact, everything is much simpler, as they say: "All ingenious is simple!" The simpler and clearer your name will be for the consumer, the better it will be perceived and the faster it will be remembered, as evidenced by the experience of world famous brands, take at least Apple.

Features of internet branding

Internet branding or e-branding is the process of preparing, developing and promoting a sustainable business image in the online field.

Brand structure

The basis of a brand is information about a product, service or company, presented in a special symbolic form, which is capable of evoking persistent associative links.

Stages of creating your own brand

Internet branding has several stages. Each of them needs to be devoted quite a lot of time and effort.

Our latest posts and rants.

Combining words.

March 19, 2021 By Sakura Haruno

Using different words or their separate parts, it is possible to compose completely new words and combinations that can be ideal for a brand name. For example, the name of the world famous sportswear brand Adidas was derived this way.

Alliteration (rhythm and rhyme).

Feb 9, 2021 By John Doe

It is much easier to remember the name if it contains rhyme, sound, certain rhythm or repetition of words. For example: Coca-Cola, M & M's, Chupa-Chups, Shake'n'Bake, etc. The Motorola name is also a combination of consonant words. According to one version, this is a derivative of "motor" - a car and "ola" - sound

Allusion or hint.

Jan 15, 2021 By Naruto Uzumaki

A method of generating a name by modifying a well-known concept, a hint of a literary or historical source, drawing parallels and analogies. For example, the name Lady X is derived from the well-known Mr. X, and the name of the Brothers Grimm musical group is the surname of the storytellers. The name of the international chain of small markets 7-Eleven also falls into this category.
